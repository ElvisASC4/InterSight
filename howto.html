Interview prep Before, during, and after

In Advance

The first thing you should do (days in advance) is research the company or organization you are applying to. Also research your interviewer if you can. Prepare questions that may be asked by the interviewer. Practice them so that when similar questions are asked you are ready. Also prepare questions to ask the interviewer to show how interested you are in the organization.

Before you arrive

You should definitely dress for success. Some jobs tell you what to wear, but if they dont business casual is always acceptable. Accessories are fine too, in moderation. Neutral colors such as black, gray, brown and blue are suitable for both men and women. You should also eat a meal that contains vitamin E, omega 3 and antioxidants. This will improve brain functionality and help you stay alert.

During

Arrive early preferably 15-25 minutes before the interview begins. This extra time can be used to practice your prepared questions and responses in your head. When you are answering questions speak clearly and with confidence. Make sure to keep your answers short and simple, and NEVER use slang.

After

When the interview is over shake hands with the interviewer(s) and thank them. After approximately 24 hours follow up with the recruiter by sending the interviewer(s) thank you notes. Most interviewers say that this impacts their decision.
